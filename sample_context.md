# My Aesthetic Taste

I'm drawn to:
- Japanese woodblock prints (ukiyo-e), especially Hiroshige's landscapes
- Minimalist architecture - Tadao Ando, John Pawson
- Ambient music - Brian Eno, Ryuichi Sakamoto
- The color palette of Wes Anderson films
- Mid-century modern furniture design

I appreciate things that feel:
- Contemplative and unhurried
- Precise but warm
- Balancing simplicity with depth
